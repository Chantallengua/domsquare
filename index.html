Are you a real
'Dom Square student'?

The Dom Square is the beating heart of Utrecht and it has a long history intertwined with student life. Eight buildings located in the famous square, in particular, can be easily associated to eight different student personality types: the relaxed one, the busy one, the pioneering one and so on.
Which kind of student are you?
Take the quiz and find out what ties you to the famous Dom Square!

Goals of the quiz

Play and educate

An interactive way to allow incoming students to learn about the history and present of their city and university.

Creating a network

Providing students with a starting point to a network of linked data, that allows them to delve deeper into topics that interest them.

Accessibility

Making Utrecht's heritage and history available to a broader audience, by providing information in English.

Connect and inspire

Inviting different kinds of students to appreciate the Dom Square and its history.

Wanna skip the quiz and browse the eight personalities?

The Dom Square is the epicentre of student activity in Utrecht and has been for almost four centuries. The university itself and many affiliated institutions have their roots on and around the square, which draws the attention of many different kinds of students.
